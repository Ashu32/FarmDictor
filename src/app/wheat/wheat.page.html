wheat Favourable Climate

Climate

  • Wheat is a rabi season crop.
  • The cool and sunny winters are very conducive for growth pf Wheat crop.
  • For getting higher yield it requires at least 100 cold days.

Temperature

  • At the time of germination it requires mean daily temperature between 20 to 25°C.
  • Mean daily temperature between 20 to 23°C accelerates the crop growth.
  • For proper grain filling it requires 23 to 25°C mean daily temperature

Crop Water Requirement

  • Equivalent to 450-650 mm of rainfall.
