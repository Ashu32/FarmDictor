sugarcane Favourable Climate

Climate

  • Hot and humid climate.
  • Warm long day produce plants with more tillers,juice and high sucrose contents.

Temperature

  • Germination stage- 30-34°C
  • Vegetation growth- 20-30°C
  • Ripening stage- 20-25°C
  • Higher temperature like 50°C stops its growth an very low temperature below 20°C slows down its growth.

Crop Water Requirement

  • Water requirement is equivalent to 1800-2200 mm of rainfall.
  • During ripening period high rainfallis not desirable because it leads to poor juice quality,formation of water shoots and increase in the tissue moisture.
