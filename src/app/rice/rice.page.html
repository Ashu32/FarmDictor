Rice Favourable Climate

Climate

  • This crop needs hot and humid climate and it is suited for region with prolonged sunshine and plenty of water.
  • More than 65 to 75 % humidity is good for rice.

Temperature

  • Rice crop needs an average temperature of 21 to 35°C
  • It can tolerate 40 to 42°C at the maximum.
  • It has been observed that each stage of cultivation process demands a different temperature range such as sprouting (10°C),Flowering (23°C),blooming (26-29°C),grain formation (21°C) and ripening (20-25°C).

Crop Water Requirement

  • Adequate and well distributed rainfall during the growing season is essential for maximum yield and quality of Rice.
  • Rice is grown in areas receiving more than 800 mm rainfall.
