maize Favourable Climate

Climate

  • Maize grows well in a wide range of climatic conditions.
  • Essential a warm weather crop.

Temperature

  • Maize crop needs an average temperature of 22 to 30°C
  • It can tolerate upto 35°C at the maximum.

Crop Water Requirement

  • Adequate soil moisture during the flowering and grain filling period is essential for maximum yield and quality of Maize.
  • Crop required water equivalent to 500-800 mm rainfall.
